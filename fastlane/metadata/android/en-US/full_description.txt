G1nkgo is a modern wallet for ẐEN, a layer-2 stablecoin built on the Ğ1 (June) libre currency blockchain (Duniter v2s).

ẐEN is the operational unit of the UPlanet cooperative ecosystem. Each Astroport relay station runs a ZEN.ECONOMY engine that automates the cooperative's constitutional rules: revenue sharing (3x1/3), fiscal provisions, and liquidity bridge between ẐEN and Euros.

Features:
- Send and receive ẐEN payments
- QR code scanning for easy transactions
- View transaction history and balance
- Biometric authentication support
- IPFS integration for decentralized content
- Market analysis tools
- Push notifications

Each G1nkgo instance connects to its UPlanet ORIGIN constellation — a network of Astroport relay nodes sharing the same swarm.key. The economic rules are defined by the cooperative's LEGAL constitution and enforced by the ZEN.ECONOMY protocol.

There are as many G1nkgo deployments as there are UPlanet ORIGIN constellations and their ẐEN clones.
